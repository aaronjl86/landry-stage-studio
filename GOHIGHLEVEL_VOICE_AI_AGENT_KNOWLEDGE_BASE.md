## 8. COMMON QUESTIONS & ANSWERS

### Pricing Questions

**Q: "Is there a free trial?"**
A: "We offer a free account where you can explore the platform and view sample staged photos. When you're ready to stage your own photos, you can choose from our three plans starting at just $29/month. Would you like me to send you a link to create your free account?"

**Q: "Can I just pay per photo instead of a subscription?"**
A: "Currently, we operate on a subscription model because it provides the best value for our clients. Even with our Starter plan at $29/month for 10 photos, that's just $2.90 per photo - far less than traditional staging. Plus, you get access to all our templates and features."

**Q: "What if I don't use all my credits in a month?"**
A: "Unfortunately, credits don't roll over month-to-month, which is why we offer multiple plan tiers. If you're consistently using fewer uploads, you might benefit from starting with our Starter plan. You can always upgrade if you need more."

### Technical Questions

**Q: "What quality are the images?"**
A: "Image quality depends on your plan: Starter provides HD quality, Professional gives you Ultra HD, and Enterprise delivers 4K resolution. All are suitable for MLS listings, websites, and marketing materials."

**Q: "Can you work with photos taken on a phone?"**
A: "Absolutely! Our AI works with photos from any source - smartphones, professional cameras, or even drone footage. For best results, we recommend well-lit photos taken at eye level."

**Q: "How long does processing take?"**
A: "Turnaround time depends on your plan: Enterprise users get 2-hour turnaround, Professional gets 12 hours, and Starter gets 24 hours. You can track the progress in real-time through your dashboard, and you'll receive an email notification when your photos are ready. The system processes up to 5 images simultaneously, so batch uploads are very efficient."

**Q: "What if I don't like the result?"**
A: "You can try different staging templates at no additional cost using the same photo. Each upload credit lets you experiment with multiple styles until you're satisfied. Our Professional and Enterprise plans also include advanced editing tools for fine-tuning. You can view before-and-after comparisons in your gallery to see which style works best."

### Service Questions

**Q: "Do you do outdoor spaces?"**
A: "Yes! We can stage outdoor areas like patios, decks, and gardens. Just select the 'Outdoor Staging' template when uploading."

**Q: "Can you remove furniture from a currently staged photo?"**
A: "Our AI specializes in adding staging to empty rooms. For furniture removal, we recommend working with photos of empty spaces for the best results."

**Q: "Do you integrate with MLS or other platforms?"**
A: "Our Enterprise plan includes API access for custom integrations. For most users, simply download the staged images and upload them to your MLS or listing platform as you normally would."

**Q: "Can multiple team members use one account?"**
A: "Our Enterprise plan includes team collaboration features. For Starter and Professional plans, accounts are designed for individual use. If you need multiple users, I can discuss Enterprise options with you."

### Business Questions

**Q: "How is this legal for real estate listings?"**
A: "Virtual staging is widely accepted in real estate, but disclosure is important. We recommend including a note like 'Photos are virtually staged to help buyers visualize the potential of the space.' Check your local MLS requirements for specific guidelines."

**Q: "Can I see examples of your work?"**
A: "Absolutely! Visit TheLandryMethod.com and you'll see multiple before-and-after examples on the homepage. I can also email you a portfolio of staged properties in your market type. What types of properties do you typically list?"

**Q: "What makes you different from other virtual staging companies?"**
A: "Three key advantages: First, our AI-powered process delivers results in hours, not days. Second, our subscription model is far more affordable than traditional per-image pricing. Third, we offer unlimited revisions on Professional and Enterprise plans, so you get exactly the look you want."

## GLOSSARY OF TERMS

**Virtual Staging**: Digitally adding furniture, decor, and design elements to photos of empty rooms

**AI Processing**: Using artificial intelligence to automatically generate staged images

**Credit**: One image upload and processing; consumed per photo

**Template**: Pre-designed staging style (Modern, Luxury, Traditional, etc.)

**Turnaround Time**: How long from upload to receiving finished staged photos

**Batch Processing**: Uploading and processing multiple images simultaneously

**Before/After**: Comparison view showing original empty room vs. staged result

**MLS**: Multiple Listing Service - database used by real estate professionals

**White-Label**: Branded as the customer's own service rather than The Landry Method

**API Access**: Technical integration allowing automated uploads from other software
